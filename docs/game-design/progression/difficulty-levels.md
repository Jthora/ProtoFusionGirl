# TCS/IDC/CBC Capacities and Capabilities Across Difficulty Levels

## Overview
In "Battle for the Heart of Humanity," the TCS/IDC/CBC is a transnational syndicate with unparalleled power, controlling global systems and intelligence agencies like the CIA. Below is a detailed mapping of their capacities and capabilities across all difficulty levels: Sandbox, Beginner, Easy, Medium, Hard, Extreme, and Impossible.

## Capacities and Capabilities
1. **Surveillance and Intelligence**: Global networks, CIA control, predictive AI, media dominance.  
2. **Economic Control**: Financial market mastery, corporate ownership, economic sabotage.  
3. **Political Influence**: Government infiltration, election rigging, blackmail via CIA data.  
4. **Technological Superiority**: Quantum computing, advanced weaponry, tech monopoly.  
5. **Social Engineering**: Social media control, educational reprogramming, PSYOPS.  
6. **Military and Paramilitary Forces**: Private armies, CIA-led operations, rapid response.  
7. **Covert Operations**: Elite assassins, counterintelligence, false flags.  
8. **Resource Control**: Resource domination, energy grid leverage, supply chain disruption.
