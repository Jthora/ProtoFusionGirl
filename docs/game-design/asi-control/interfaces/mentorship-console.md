# The Mentorship Console Interface: Relationship-Focused Guidance

## Overview

The Mentorship Console Interface transforms the player-character relationship from controller-puppet into mentor-student, creating deep emotional bonds that transcend traditional gaming experiences. Rather than commanding Jane directly, players nurture her growth, provide wisdom during challenging moments, and witness her development into an independent, capable individual. This interface creates profound emotional investment that makes players prefer guidance over control.

## Core Design Philosophy

### Visual Metaphor
The interface draws inspiration from:
- **Teacher-Student Relationships**: The wisdom-sharing dynamic between mentor and apprentice
- **Therapy Sessions**: Safe spaces for emotional growth and personal development
- **Parental Guidance**: Protective yet empowering support for personal growth
- **Coaching Interfaces**: Performance improvement through guidance and feedback

### Psychological Impact
Players should feel they are:
- **Wise Mentors**: Providing guidance based on superior knowledge and experience
- **Emotional Guardians**: Protecting Jane's mental and emotional wellbeing
- **Growth Facilitators**: Helping Jane become more than she could be alone
- **Trusted Advisors**: Building relationships based on mutual respect and understanding

The Mentorship Console Interface creates a fundamentally different relationship between player and character, transforming control into care, commands into guidance, and manipulation into mentorship. This deep emotional investment makes players prefer the rich, complex relationship of mentorship over the simple, direct control of traditional gaming, contributing significantly to the "ASI withdrawal" effect that makes cosmic intelligence feel superior to avatar control.
